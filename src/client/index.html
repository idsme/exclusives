Luxuriously furnished penthouse (at a 100 meters above see-level, 32-floor) with breath taking view on three sides, over looking the Rotterdam Skyline and surrounding up to 20kms on every side. The Schieland Tower is the highest brick apartment tower in the Netherlands, at a 100 meters you will be dining and sleeping on cloud nine. The “Schieland Tower’s” is uniquely situated within Rotterdam’s heart, it is literally located in absolute city-center, city-hall, clubs, the movie-theater and the more exclusive shopping streets are all reachable within a few minutes. All methods of public transports like trams, busses, the “Central Trains Station & Blaak” are at your disposal.

Upon entering the spacious living room from the hallway you will probably directly be drawn to the view at the windows. After a few minutes at the windows you may start to notice the +- 40 square meters living room including 2 couches perfectly setup for an evening of television, a table to seat 6 and an open kitchen which allows for perfect interaction with guests when having friends over. The apartment offers 2 stylish bedrooms with a double bed and amazing view (in the direction of city hall). There is even a room where you can change after showers and setup a laptop for work purposes. Last but not least the bathroom has been enlarged, redesigned and it now contains a large walk-in shower, a extra large bath for relaxing after a hard day’s work. To perfectly start of your adventure and enjoy the pleasure of city-live, I can highly recommend eating out for as many times as you time allows. Streets like “De witte de withstraat” en de “Meent” will be a good starting point.

Fact-sheet: 

Schieland Tower is equipped with two high speed elevators taking you from the ground floor to your doorstep at a +- a 100 meters heigh in just 40 seconds. The apartment comes with some extra store space located on the 8th floor and an in apartment storage room next to the front door. A janitor makes sure that the buildings equipment is in working order and all-floors are kept clean.

The apartment is available for shorts stay:

Parking spots can be rented in the parking garage attached to the Schieland Tower or at a reduced rates nearby.

Al that remains is for you to request a viewing thus taking the first step in the direction of an unforgettable experience.
