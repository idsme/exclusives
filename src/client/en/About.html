About us

Rotterdam Apartments B.V.
Rotterdam Apartments B.V. a subdivision of Rotterdam Relocation and Rotterdam Estate, a Group of integrated companies which offer services for business professionals from all over the world who work on projects in the Netherlands.

We support customers emigrating to the Netherlands, search for possible accommodations and rentals for periods of a few days to a few years.

Our experienced international team knows the problems HR managers face. We help you match the company policy for international moving and our service offering facilitates a quick and financially attractive moving process, which allows you to focus on your core business.

Rotterdam Apartments B.V.

Rotterdam Apartments B.V. offers furnished and unfurnished rooms, studios, apartments, houses and villas with prices starting around 400 euros per month. We have over 300 properties available for rent.

Short stay

Rotterdam Apartments B.V. serviced apartments are perfect for the (business) traveller staying in Rotterdam for at least one month and who wishes to have all the comforts and ease of an apartment during their stay.
