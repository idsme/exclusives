Beautifully furnished penthouse in the center of Rotterdam with three floors on 22nd, 23rd and 24th floor, three bedrooms and a fantastic view of the city skyline. Right in the center and within walking distance of the market hall, shops, restaurants and train station Blaak. Available for three months!



The complex is situated on the unique and surrounded by water Wijnhaveneiland at the foot of the Meuse. Is off the street on the Blaak center where all amenities are within walking distance. The complex has a representative entrance with elevators and videophone. There is also a private (bicycle) storage in the basement there. There is a caretaker available daily.

Entrance of the house, with toilet area, directly competing in the spacious kitchen with floor to ceiling windows allowing maximum visibility and light. Also from the side windows of the luxury kitchen (with ceramic hob, dishwasher and oven), you have a fantastic view towards the river. Large storage room and stairs to the top floor.

From the kitchen you take the stairs down to the spacious living room which adjoins the spacious roof terrace with views of downtown and the city skyline.

The top floor provides access to two bedrooms with large windows and a third bedroom which is used as a study. On this floor there is also a luxurious bathroom with two sinks and a shower / steam room.
