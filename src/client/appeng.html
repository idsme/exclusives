In Rotterdam Zuid located furnished room with shared living room and kitchen. In addition there are two toilets and bathrooms present that must be shared. Nearby is Zuidplein shoppingmall, tram 25 and bus 66, 77.
Layout: 
Shared entrance. Entrance to the third floor. The room is furnished with two single beds but can also be pushed together and is equipped with fridge and washbasin.
